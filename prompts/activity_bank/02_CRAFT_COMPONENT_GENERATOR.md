# Craft Component Generator

For PAPER_CRAFT, CUT_AND_BUILD, and PRAYER_OR_GRATITUDE_CRAFT pages:

* list every cuttable part with a clear label
* include cut-line and fold-line guidance
* keep shapes simple enough for ages 6–13
* require parent supervision note when scissors or glue are used
* connect every part to a named story object or moment
* never invent peacock feathers except for Krishna himself
