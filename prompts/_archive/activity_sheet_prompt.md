# Activity Sheet Prompt

Generate story-specific activity sheet content for ages 6–12.

Include:

- 3 recall questions
- 2 thinking questions
- 10 word-search words from the story
- 1 draw/color activity tied to the scene
- 1 simple family activity or craft
- 5-star challenge checklist (5 items)
- Parent reminder to send photo/audio/video after completion

Do not use generic placeholders unrelated to the current Krishna Book pastime.
