# What is Bhāva?

Bhāva is an independent curated **devotional learning** portal stewarded by Svarna Gauranga Das in the Harrisburg, Pennsylvania area.

It begins with child-friendly **Krishna Book** bedtime stories for children, parents, teachers, Sunday Schools, and preachers.

AI may assist production, while **source fidelity** and human review remain required before public release.

Bhāva is **not** an official BBT publication and does not republish full copyrighted books.
