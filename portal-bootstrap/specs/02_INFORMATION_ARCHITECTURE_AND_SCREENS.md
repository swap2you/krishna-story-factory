## Child-friendly interaction rules

- Minimum 44px touch targets
- Plain-language labels
- No advertising
- No public comments
- No child accounts in v1
- No dark patterns
- Calm, purposeful animation
- Audio-first story pages
- Device-local bookmarks, playlists, progress and notes
