# Krishna Book Bedtime Content Quality Rules

1. Audience: children ages 6–12.
2. Tone: warm, devotional, simple, respectful, vivid.
3. Do not invent new scripture claims.
4. Do not include graphic violence.
5. Do not over-explain philosophy.
6. Always include source reference.
7. Always explain Sanskrit names simply.
8. Make the story feel like bedtime Krishna-katha, not a school essay.
9. Use short paragraphs.
10. Include sensory details, but no cheap fantasy.
11. Include a parent note with source and discussion suggestion.
12. Avoid clumsy generic morals.
13. Moral must directly come from the pastime.
14. Five-star challenge must be practical for children.
15. Activity sheet must be story-specific, not generic.
