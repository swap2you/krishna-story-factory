# Content Safety Guide

This project creates devotional content for children ages 7–11.

## Required content style

- Child-friendly.
- Krishna-conscious.
- Faithful to the source reference.
- Simple language.
- Sweet but not childish.
- Clear moral and practical takeaway.

## Avoid

- Graphic violence.
- Adult themes.
- Sectarian attacks.
- Invented scriptural claims.
- Copying copyrighted stories word-for-word.
- Copying Amar Chitra Katha panels, captions, or layout.

## Required sections in every story

- Recap
- Main Story
- Moral
- Takeaway
- Five-Star Challenge

## Recommended devotional structure

Use hearing, chanting, remembering, service, prayer, friendship with Krishna, and offering with love as recurring themes.
